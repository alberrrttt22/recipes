Air-Fried Broccoli

Delicious broccoli that tastes like loklok broccoli (if you haven't tried loklok broccoli, please go try them now. Please.)

Ingredients

  • Broccoli
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Herbs and Spices
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Cut the broccoli into smaller pieces and remove the stems
  2. Wash the broccoli
  3. Add salt, pepper, herbs and spices and drizzle with olive oil (make sure each broccoli is coated)
  4. Put the broccoli into oven for 10 min on high heat
  5. The broccoli will now be crispy and flavorful
  6. Plate and serve! Enjoy it as a quick snack or make a big batch and store for future consumption (meal prep)
