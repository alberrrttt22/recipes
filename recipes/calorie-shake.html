1000 Calorie Shake

Gainss. Can be drunk as a breakfast shake or after/before workout shake. Or however you like

PS: will probably not look like the picture

This shake has 1000 calories and 77.4g protein

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 2 tbs peanut butter
  • 50g Rolled oats
  • 1 tbs chia seeds
  • 2 scoops protein powder
  • 1 tbs runny honey
  • 300ml full cream milk
  • 200ml water

Steps

  1. Blend the oats to a smooth consistency
  2. Add 1 tbs chia seed, 2 scoops protein powder, 2 tbs peanut butter, 300ml milk and 1 tbs runny honey to the container with the blended oats.
  3. Break up the banana into smaller pieces and add it in followed by 200ml water
  4. Blend it all together
